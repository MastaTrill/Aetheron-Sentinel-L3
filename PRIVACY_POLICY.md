# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 13, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 13, 2026

Aetheron Sentinel ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and protect your personal data when you use our services, website, and contact forms.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Contact Form & Service Requests
When you submit a contact form or request our services, we collect:

- **Name** - Your full name or organization name.
- **Email Address** - For service delivery and communication.
- **Company Name** - To understand your organization.
- **Service Type** - Which service you're interested in (audit, networking, code analysis, APY enhancement).
- **Message Content** - Your specific questions or requirements.

### Website & Telemetry
We collect:

- **Usage Data** - Pages visited, time spent, browser type, IP address (for security purposes only).
- **Cookies** - Optional analytics cookies (if enabled by user preference).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data solely for:

- **Service Delivery** - Responding to audit requests, providing quotes, and delivering services.
- **Communication** - Sending service updates, confirmations, and support responses.
- **Security** - Detecting fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access attempts.
- **Compliance** - Meeting legal obligations and maintaining audit trails.

**We do not:**
- Sell or rent your personal data to third parties.
- Share your email address or company information with external companies.
- Use your data for marketing without explicit consent.
- Build user profiles beyond what is necessary for service delivery.

## 3. Data Retention

- **Contact Form Data** - Retained for 90 days after your last interaction, then deleted.
- **Service Request Data** - Retained for the duration of the engagement plus 1 year (for compliance and dispute resolution).
- **Audit Reports & Findings** - Retained per your Service Level Agreement terms.
- **Usage Logs** - Deleted after 30 days except where required for security investigations.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing aetheron.solana@gmail.com.

## 4. Data Security

- Personal data is stored on secure, encrypted servers.
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit.
- We conduct regular security audits and maintain incident response procedures (see [SECURITY_POLICY.md](SECURITY_POLICY.md)).

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers for:

- **Email Services** - Sending service communications.
- **Analytics** - Understanding website usage (anonymized).
- **Cloud Infrastructure** - Hosting data securely.

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for our benefit.
