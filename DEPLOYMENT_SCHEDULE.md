# Deployment and Handover Schedule

## Overview

This schedule outlines the deployment timeline and handover process for Aetheron Sentinel L3 integration with BMNR systems. The deployment is planned for a phased rollout to minimize risk and ensure thorough testing.

## Timeline Overview

### Phase 1: Pre-Deployment Preparation (Week 1-2)

**Start Date**: May 1, 2026
**Duration**: 2 weeks
**Objectives**: Complete all integration testing and documentation

### Phase 2: Testnet Deployment (Week 3)

**Start Date**: May 15, 2026
**Duration**: 1 week
**Objectives**: Deploy to testnet and validate integration

### Phase 3: Mainnet Deployment (Week 4)

**Start Date**: May 22, 2026
**Duration**: 1 week
**Objectives**: Production deployment with monitoring

### Phase 4: Post-Deployment Support (Ongoing)

**Start Date**: May 29, 2026
**Duration**: 4 weeks minimum
**Objectives**: Monitor, optimize, and handover to operations

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

- **Rollback Plan**: Complete system rollback capability
- **Backup Systems**: Redundant monitoring and alerting
- **Testing Environments**: Multiple test environments available

### Operational Risks

- **Communication Plan**: Multiple channels for updates
- **Escalation Paths**: Clear decision-making hierarchy
- **Resource Allocation**: Dedicated teams for each phase

### Business Risks

- **Contingency Budget**: Additional funds for delays
- **Vendor Backup**: Alternative providers identified
- **Insurance Coverage**: Incident response insurance in place

## Communication Plan

### Internal Communication

- **Daily Standups**: 9:00 AM EDT during deployment weeks
- **Status Reports**: Hourly during deployment, daily otherwise
- **Escalation Matrix**: Clear paths for issue escalation

### External Communication

- **Stakeholder Updates**: Weekly progress reports
- **Public Announcements**: Coordinated messaging
- **User Notifications**: Pre-deployment and post-deployment notices

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Performance benchmarks met

### Business Success

- [ ] Stakeholder approval obtained
